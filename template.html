Hello There

Welcome to our redesigned fortnightly update! Our Creative and Communications Streams have been hard at work and TYN is thrilled to be bringing you updates in this new format.

The Creative team has also been busily preparing for our relaunch of The Youth Network Times (TYNT), our youth publication. TYNT has been revamped to offer content in a fresh format that's more easily accessible on-the-go while still maintaining its stunning visuals. I've seen a preview and I can't wait for the launch later this month!

Meanwhile, enjoy all the other news below... August will be an exciting month for TYN!

Anthony (Chair of the Youth Network)
