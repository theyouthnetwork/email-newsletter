Hello There

Welcome to our redesigned fortnightly update! Our Creative and Communications Streams have been hard at work and TYN is thrilled to be bringing you updates in this new format.

The Creative team has also been busily preparing for our relaunch of The Youth Network Times (TYNT), our youth publication. TYNT has been revamped to offer content in a fresh format that's more easily accessible on-the-go while still maintaining its stunning visuals. I've seen a preview and I can't wait for the launch later this month!

Meanwhile, enjoy all the other news below... August will be an exciting month for TYN!

Anthony (Chair of the Youth Network)

Spotlight News

What's been happening?

As we are busily getting TYNT ready for launch in two weeks, we thought we would give you a snapshot into what you can expect to hear from us. With every new update, you will get a preview of our best new stories here first. Following positive reviews last year, we will be bringing back Humans of TYNT where we will reveal the diversity of those who work alongside us. We will also put a greater focus on content that drives conversation and thoughts which includes thought leadership, self-development and innovative ideas. We want to be the place that you go to share and express your thoughts and ideas, become educated on the things that matter most to Youth and catch-up on key content from all our events.

To be one of the first to see the new face of TYNT, be sure to register using the survey link in this newsletter!
